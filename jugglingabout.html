About Me

I started juggling when I was 8 years old.

  • 2005

    Humble Beginnings

    In highschool, my calculus teacher also hosted a juggling club and for some reason I felt right at home. I learned to juggle clubs and pass with other jugglers.

  • 2014

    Traveling

    I took a trip around the world with my juggling clubs. I busked in London, Paris, Bangkok and Sydney.

  • 2017

    TreeHouse Tribe

    My friends asked me to be a main character in a kids show they were producing! We shot the pilot over a weekend in some treehouses in West Texas.

  • 2017

    Joining Circus Picnic

    The TreeHouse Tribe crew transitioned into Circus Picnic, event production and bringing the circus to private events!

  • 2020 - Now

    Street Performing

    My juggling partner and I perform at private parties, events and even on the street!
