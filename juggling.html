"Juggling is mesmorizing to witness, and meditative to practice!"

Eric Summers

Juggler

Eric has been juggling since he was in middleschool. In highschool, Eric's calculus teacher hosted a weekly juggling club. It was there that Eric learned to juggle clubs and club passing. He soon realized that people were quite entertained with his juggling, and soon began performing on the street with this juggling friends. Years later, he joined CircusPicnic and performed with them at festivals for many years. Eric has performed at countless events for numerous entertainment production companies. Eric has taught hundreds of people to juggle over the years. Sharing the art of juggling, whether performing or teaching, is one of Eric's many passions in life.

All Levels

All experience levels are welcome. Whether you are new to juggling, or ready to level-up, lessons are tailored to meet your skill level. Eric has been teaching juggling to teens and adults for over 10 years!

Different Locations
